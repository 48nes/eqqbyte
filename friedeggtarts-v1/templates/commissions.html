Static sites are sites like these! They can be used as a portfolio site, as a replacement for carrd, a place to host your comic, or anything else you might think of. Static sites do not interact with the viewer in any way, if you are interested in Dynamic Sites, check out the other tab.

terms of service

  1. The commission does not include hosting fees, custom domains, or setup*.
  2. Payment will be done in halves, half upfront and half paid once you have approved the preview site. You will receive all the site files once I have received the second half of the payment.
  3. I currently accept payments through Paypal only.
  4. I do not refund payments unless I am the one to cancel the commission.
  5. Do not message me about the commission except for check-in dates/times.

*I recommend Netlify, Github Pages, and Heroku for hosting.

process

  1. Send me a message (Discord preferred) with "code commission" somewhere in the original message.
  2. In your message, please include a mockup of every page you will need for your website. For static site commissions, I will not work off of descriptions only. Your mockup can be a very simple sketch with text descriptions, but I must have an image mockup to work from.
  3. I will give you a price quote, an approximate time-frame for the project, and a list of questions. Once the price is verified and the questions answered, I will send an invoice for the first half. Please pay this within ten days.
  4. I will have 2-10 progress checks depending on the site's complexity. During this time, please let me know any change you want made. There is a cap on progress checks, so please be thorough during each.
  5. Once all progress checks have been completed, I will send the invoice for the second half of the invoice. Please pay this within ten days.
  6. Once I have received the payment, I will email you the files.

pricing

My starting rate is $100, with $50 for each additional page. If the site requires complex JavaScript, the price may be higher.
