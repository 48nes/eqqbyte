bio

Ten was the firstborn in the Akkad clan and was trained to head the family once she became of age. However, at the age of 16, Ten decided to leave the family and explore Edan instead. Stripped of her titles, Ten narrowly managed to escape to Terres, where she encountered Cael. The two, despite starting off poorly, quickly became friends. Ten, after her recovery, attempted to convince Cael to travel with her to the central city of Terres, but Cael refused. The two continued to stay in touch after Ten moved, through letters. Ten also secretly sent letters to her cousin, Arya, during her expeditions in Edan.
